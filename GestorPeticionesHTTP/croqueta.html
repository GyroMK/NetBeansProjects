HexaBEAT!

HexaBEAT! is a thrilling rhythm game that puts your timing to the test. Control a hexagon with three colored faces, moving to the beat of the music. As you progress, the challenges ramp up, offering an addictive blend of skill and rhythm.

SONGS

You can find more songs on our Discord, created by the community. As developers, we don't make or share the songs ourselves. Instead, community members can share their creations and songs through the Discord community to be played in the game. In the future, we plan to manually add selected songs to the game.
